WIPRO IS HIRING FOR DIPLOMA GRADUATES


WIPRO is offering jobs to Diploma graduates in their organisation.The details of the job requirement given below

JOB DESCRIPTION:

SCHOLAR TRAINEE
Transform your career with Wipro SIM - School for IT Infrastructure Management.
This uniquely tailored programme helps you build a high-flying career.
You can now upgrade your diploma to a Wipro sponsored B. Tech in Information Systems from a university accorded "Institute of Eminence" (IoE) status by the Government of India, while you work as a full-time employee with Wipro. Kickstart your career, join us as 'Scholar Trainee - SIM'!

ELIGIBILITY:

1.Diploma in Computer Science/ Information Technology/ Electronics/ Telecommunication
2.2020, 2021 and 2022 Passed out Students
3.Must be having 60% and above throughout the academics.
4.Maximum of 3 years of education gap, if any, is allowed between 10th and graduation.
5.No gaps are allowed during graduation. The degree should be completed within 3 years from the start of the course.
6.Education should be regular full-time in 10th, 12th & graduation recognized by the Central/State Government of India.
7.Candidates with part-time, correspondence or distance learning in either 10th or 12th or graduation are not eligible.
8.Maximum of one backlog till the assessment process is allowed. The offer will be subject to all backlogs being cleared at the time of offer.
9.Should be an Indian Citizen or should hold a PIO or OCI card in the event of holding a passport of any other country.
10.Bhutan and Nepal Nationals need to submit their citizenship certificate.
11.Candidates who have participated in any selection process held by Wipro in the last three months are not eligible.


COMPENSATION PER ANNUM:

1.First-year stipend: INR 12,400 per month
2.Second-year stipend: INR 15,488 per month
3.Third-year stipend: INR 17,553 per month
4.Fourth-year stipend: INR 19,618 per month



Selection Process
1.Online Test
2.Business Round


SERVICE AGREEMENT: The agreement period is of 48 months from the date of joining. The candidate will be liable to pay a sum of INR 75,000 on a pro-rata basis if he/she leaves the organization before the completion of 48 months for breaching the service agreement.
